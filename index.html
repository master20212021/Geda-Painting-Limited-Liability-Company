About Geda Painting

Trusted Residential Work, Built for Real Homes

Geda Painting LLC is a New York limited liability company registered since 2016, built on 30 years of residential painting experience. The company focuses only on occupied homes, where careful prep, clean protection, and dependable execution matter most.

From walls, kitchens, bedrooms, and baths to exterior siding, trim, doors, and windows, every project is approached with detailed spackle work, sanding, repair, and high-quality finishing so the house feels renewed inside and out.

Perfect for homeowners who want the house fresh, protected, and ready for the summer season without dealing with a commercial-style contractor experience.
